üéØ Key Points to Remember

  • Multiple differences: If the difference is ‚Ç¨0.02, distribute ‚Ç¨0.01 to the two largest items
  • Order matters: Always adjust items in order of value (largest to smallest)
  • Multiple VAT rates: Apply this process separately for each VAT rate group
  • Negative differences: If difference is negative, subtract from the largest item
  • Alternative approach: You can adjust VAT amounts instead of Net amounts (same result)
  • Always verify: Final totals should always match the total method calculation

üìù Summary

The goal is to compare totals and distribute cent differences across line items to move the cents that have shifted from Net to VAT (or vice versa) due to rounding.

This ensures your line-by-line breakdown matches your total calculations perfectly, which is crucial for accounting and compliance.
